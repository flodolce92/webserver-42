GALACTIK FOOTBALL

Experience the ultimate sport across the galaxy where teams from different planets compete using The Breath - mystical powers unique to each world.

The Universe

In the distant future, football has evolved into the galaxy's most popular sport. Teams from across the universe compete in the prestigious Galactik Football Cup, each wielding The Breath - supernatural powers connected to their home planet's essence.

The Snow Kids

Follow the journey of Akillian's team, the Snow Kids, as they rise from underdogs to galactic champions. Led by the talented D'Jok and guided by coach Aarch, they must master the Breath of Akillian to compete against the galaxy's elite teams.

The Breath

Each planet possesses a unique form of The Breath that grants special abilities during matches. From Akillian's ice powers to Genesis's lightning, these mystical forces transform football into an spectacular cosmic sport unlike anything seen before.

Three Epic Seasons

Season 1: The Awakening

The Snow Kids discover their powers and compete in their first Galactik Football Cup, facing formidable opponents and uncovering ancient secrets.

Season 2: The Revenge

With new challenges and enemies, the Snow Kids must defend their title while dealing with the mysterious corruption affecting The Breath across the galaxy.

Season 3: Revolution

The final season brings revelations about the true nature of The Breath and the ultimate test for our heroes as they face their greatest challenge yet.
